Welcome to Boy Scout Troop 10!

Troop 10 is dedicated to providing adventurous and meaningful experiences for all of our scouts. Our goal is to foster leadership, community service, and outdoor skills.

About Us

Troop 10 is a group of over 60 boys and 90 adults who come together to learn, grow, and develop into responsible and dedicated individuals. We focus on outdoor activities, leadership training, and community service projects that help scouts become well-rounded citizens.

Where Do We Go

Troop 10 has weekly meetings on mondays at 7:00 pm. We meet at the Church of the Holy Comforter. Troop 10 also has outdoor outings almost every month and we got to many place. To name a few, Battle of Aiken in Aiken County, Sliding Rock in North Carolina, and summer camps like Bert Adams in Newton County.
